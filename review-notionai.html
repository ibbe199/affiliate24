What Is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an AI assistant integrated directly inside Notion's workspace. It helps users write content, summarize notes, brainstorm ideas, and automate repetitive documentation tasks.

Unlike standalone AI tools, Notion AI works directly inside documents and team workspaces.
