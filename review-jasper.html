What Is Jasper?

Jasper is an AI-powered writing assistant designed for marketers, bloggers, and businesses. It helps generate blog posts, sales copy, emails, ad creatives, and long-form content using advanced AI models.

Unlike basic AI tools, Jasper focuses heavily on marketing workflows and ready-made templates.

Key Features

  • Long-form Blog Writing
  • SEO Mode (with Surfer integration)
  • Brand Voice Customization
  • Team Collaboration
